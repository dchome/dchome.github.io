Daniel Homer

Full-Stack Web Developer

I grew up in the outskirts of Dover, Delaware, between an Amish farm and a golf course. I have always loved figuring out how things work and learning new skills.

I attended the College of William and Mary, where I studied philosophy and economics (and graduated with highest honors). Then I got a JD from the University of Virginia School of Law.

I worked as a litigation attorney at top firms in Delaware and New York. The job required extremely strong oral and written communication skills, problem-solving, and the ability to work as part of a team, all in a very high-pressure environment.

When I was ready for my next challenge I began exploring web development. In early 2016 I attended DevBootCamp, a 19-week intensive web development program. Since then, I've been building websites and apps and writing clean, elegant code.
